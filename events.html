DOUBLE HAPPINESS

A new live concert platform, Eastern Standard Time (EST) is scheduled to launch via its first event - the inaugural DOUBLE HAPPINESS Winter Festival.
Set to take place on 2 December, 7pm EST (8am SGT/PHT) or (7am TH/ID Standard Time), the livestream event will kick off the holiday season with performances from global artis
The festival will be available for streaming via EST for free. The lineup includes the likes of Ardhito Pramono, CHAI, Seori, CHUNG HA, Yuna, ATEEZ, Pamungkas, Ylona Garcia, Chi Pu, Balming Tiger, NxWorries (Anderson. Paak and Knxwledge), and many more.
